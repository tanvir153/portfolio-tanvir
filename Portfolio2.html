About Me

Md Tanvir Sardarr

From my childhood, I was always mesmerized by how simple hi-tech appliances made our daily activities smoother and efficient to carry on. Science of Computer has become a well-known subsets & untapped area which is filled with the humongous potential to change the world. So, I was always fascinated by this omnipresence of computer tools and gadgets as well as curious in exploring the inner-workings of the daily appliances we use.

Therefore, I completed my High School & College education in science background. As an ardent gaming fan and tech geek, I started learning coding first during 12th grade and leapt into the world of computer programming. After finishing my college, I got an opportunity to study Bachelor Degree in Software Engineering with a Chinese Local Govt Scholarship in China. After that there has been no looking back till finishing my bachelor degree. After pursuing Bachelor Degree, I aim to improve & strength my knowledge & strive to acquire the necessary skills of this powerful blend of technology.

During my bachelor’s study, I have built several personal projects using Python, Html, Java-Script, Java & their technologies like Django & MongoDB. Some of my JavaScript projects are Weather App, Music Event App, Photo Details Display, A Shopping Cart for Order Fulfilment. For graduation project I have built an Online Doctor Consultant web app using Python & Django framework.

Although I don’t have any professional work experience yet but I always try to devote myself to learn. As far as possible I like to do self-exploration and self-empowerment. My curiosity to know about operations and applications led me obtain my bachelor degree with a CGPA of 3.50 out of 4 with an excellent average score of 87.2%.

During this period, I also got an Out- Standing Student Award on 2019-2020 academic year from Zhengzhou University. Besides I have completed HSK-5 which consider as the advance Chinese Language level.

I really enjoy reading Fiction books, especially those that are very imaginary. I do prefer to read computer magazines as I enjoy keeping up to date with new technology and gadgets.
